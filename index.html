Core Marketing Principles

  1. TikTok drives curiosity. Pinterest builds trust. Etsy/Shopify converts. Every decision serves that flow.
  2. Educate first, sell second. Scholar history slides outperform product videos. The product appears naturally at the end.
  3. Test before scaling. One product, one content type at a time. Double down on what data shows working.
  4. Seasonal content leads by 4-6 weeks. Q4 content starts mid-September. Pinterest seasonal pins start October for December buyers.
  5. Every product launch = TikTok + Pinterest + Email. All three fire together.

  1. Year in Letters is your breakout product. The concept — 12 sealed letters, one opened per month — is visual, emotional, instantly understood. Film it well and post December 1.
  2. Language collections are your long-term moat. No other stationery brand in the dark academia niche is doing Biblical Hebrew or Koine Greek journals. This differentiation builds a loyal audience no competitor can easily copy.
  3. Scholar history slides built your audience. Every new product launch should be preceded by a scholar slide that connects thematically to that product. Educate first, sell second — always.
